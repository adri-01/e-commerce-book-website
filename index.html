Booklist

  • The Bro Code

    The Bro Code is a humor book inspired by the TV show How I Met Your Mother and written by Matt Kuhn. It presents a fictional “code” of rules that bros should follow in friendship, dating, and everyday life. Narrated by the character Barney Stinson, the book mixes comedy, exaggerated advice, and absurd life lessons. The rules are intentionally over-the-top and meant for entertainment rather than serious guidance. Fans enjoy it for its witty style, references to the show, and playful take on male friendships and social behavior.
